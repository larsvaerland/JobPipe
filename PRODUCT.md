# Product

## Primary user

A job seeker who is overwhelmed by job volume, weak relevance, and inconsistent follow-up.

## Root problems

1. Too many irrelevant job listings
2. Too much time spent on first-pass screening
3. Weak structure in application follow-up
4. Poor traceability in why jobs were chosen or skipped

## Wedge

Solve triage and prioritization first.

## What Jobpipe is solving first

Jobpipe is focused on helping one person run a better job search workflow:
- reduce low-value review work
- prioritize more consistently
- keep status and decisions visible
- create a more structured process from discovery to follow-up

## Non-goals

- Full ATS replacement
- Generic CV builder
- Multi-user SaaS in v1
- Fully autonomous applying
- Feature bloat for its own sake

## Product principles

- Use cheap filters before expensive AI calls
- Keep decisions traceable
- Support human judgment
- Prefer practical value over complexity
- Build one strong workflow before expanding scope

## Success

Jobpipe is succeeding when it:
- reduces time spent reviewing low-fit jobs
- improves the quality of jobs that reach apply stage
- makes follow-up easier to manage
- becomes easier for another person to understand and use
