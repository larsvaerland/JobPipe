# T001 - Dual-Client Instruction Consolidation

## Goal

Consolidate JobPipe's dual-client AI instruction setup into the correct
canonical locations without turning root instruction files into large catch-all
notes.

## Why this exists

The repo now has Codex and Claude config, agents, skills, and orchestration
templates. The useful rules need to live in the right places:

- role-specific behavior in root instruction files and agent definitions
- shared process in `docs/ai-playbook.md`
- live state in `docs/current-state.json`
- durable decisions in `docs/decisions.md`
- task-specific plans in `docs/execplans/`

## Scope

- Keep `AGENTS.md` short and Codex-specific.
- Keep `CLAUDE.md` short and Claude-specific.
- Preserve `PRODUCT_VISION.md` as canonical product vision.
- Keep `docs/vision.md` as a short AI-facing adapter.
- Move shared workflow rules into `docs/ai-playbook.md`.
- Keep `docs/current-state.json` limited to live operational state.
- Record durable placement and workflow decisions in `docs/decisions.md`.
- Keep role behavior in `.codex/agents/`, `.claude/agents/`, `.agents/skills/`,
  and `.claude/skills/`.

## Out of Scope

- Deleting historical files.
- Changing user-level Codex or Claude config.
- Creating or removing worktrees.
- Runtime pipeline behavior changes.
- New dependencies.
- Moving private or candidate-specific prompt material into public canonical docs.
