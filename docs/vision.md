## Product

JobPipe helps privacy-conscious job seekers turn noisy job-market data into evidence-backed decisions and focused next actions.

## Priorities

1. Reliability over novelty
2. Small reversible changes
3. Clear user flow over clever abstraction

## Non-goals

- no unrelated rewrites
- no speculative architecture changes
- no broad refactors during narrow fixes
